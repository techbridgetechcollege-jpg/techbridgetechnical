KNEC ICT Programmes

🏛️ Kenya National Examinations Council — Accredited ICT Courses

Techbridge Technical College offers KNEC-registered ICT programmes that are nationally recognised across Kenya. These qualifications are examined and certified by the Kenya National Examinations Council, giving graduates credentials trusted by employers in both the public and private sector.

Our KNEC ICT courses combine theory examinations with practical laboratory work, ensuring graduates have both the knowledge and the hands-on skills required for employment and further study.

Cybersecurity & Ethical Hacking

Kenya's most practical cybersecurity training — real tools, real scenarios, hackathon-style CTF labs, and industry-recognised exam preparation.

Learn by Hacking.
Defend by Knowing.

Our CTF (Capture The Flag) programme places students in real-world attack and defence scenarios. No theory-only sessions — every module is built around hands-on labs, real targets in isolated environments, and the practical skills that security employers demand.

Join the Cyber Programme
🌐 Web Application Hacking
SQLi, XSS, SSRF, CSRF, IDOR — OWASP Top 10 in real app environments
🔒 Cryptography Labs
Breaking and implementing real-world encryption and hashing algorithms
🧩 Reverse Engineering
Binary analysis, malware dissection, Ghidra and GDB
🕵️ Digital Forensics
File carving, memory analysis, steganography, and evidence handling
🖥️ Privilege Escalation
